Step-by-step flow

From first call to ongoing optimization — designed to reduce chaos.

1) Sales call

We confirm your market, criteria, offer language, and lead definition. If there’s a mismatch, we’ll say it clearly.

2) Onboarding

Access, tracking expectations, and a simple plan for the first 2–4 weeks so you know what “progress” looks like early.

3) Setup

Scripts/templates, list rules, routing, and quality checks. We build the execution system before volume.

4) Launch

Daily execution begins. We monitor early signals: contact rate, conversation quality, objections, and lead flow.

5) Weekly reporting

Every week you get a snapshot: What happened, What we learned, and What we’ll do next. Clear numbers, calm commentary.

6) Optimization

We adjust scripts, segments, cadence, and list rules based on real conversations — then measure the effect.

Weekly snapshot format (example)

A consistent format so you can scan quickly and stay confident in the process.

  • Topline metrics (contact rate, conversations, leads)
  • Objections + what’s changing next week
  • Notes on list quality + recommended segments
