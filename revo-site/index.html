Who this is for (and who it isn’t)

Clear fit saves time and protects outcomes.

Great fit if you…

  • Want a managed operation, not “random callers”
  • Have a defined market + acquisition criteria
  • Can follow up quickly on warm leads
  • Prefer weekly reporting and steady improvements
  • Care about brand tone and consistency

Not a fit if you…

  • Expect instant deals in the first few days
  • Don’t have time to follow up or close leads
  • Want ultra-cheap volume with no QA
  • Change markets/criteria every week
  • Don’t want data or feedback loops

FAQ

Clear answers — so expectations stay aligned.

Do you provide the callers/texters?

We run the operation and can manage dedicated reps as part of the engagement. Your plan is structured around consistent execution and reporting.

How quickly can we launch?

Most launches happen after onboarding and setup. Timeline depends on list readiness, scripts, and access to tools.

What makes REVO “premium execution”?

We focus on repeatable systems: QA, script consistency, tracking, weekly snapshots, and an optimization cadence you can rely on.

What results should I expect?

Early wins often look like improved contact rates, more real conversations, and clearer objections. Deals depend on market, list quality, follow-up, and offer.

Do you do acquisitions / close deals?

No — we focus on lead generation ops. You (or your acquisitions team) handle underwriting, offers, and closing.
